Never use em dashes ( — ) in blog posts or any content. Use commas or colons for pauses instead.

**Why:** David finds em dashes unnatural. "Humans don't use those."

**How to apply:** Replace every em dash with a comma or colon depending on context. Applies to all written content: blog posts, descriptions, meta tags, README files.
